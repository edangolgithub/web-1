Gintama

Gintama is a japanese anime series from the manga of the same name made by the author Sorachi Hideaki in early 2000's. Gintama is a comedy, action show with shounen demograph. Its extremely loved anime in Japan and even outside Japan.

Its tells the fictional story of Edo Japan where the alien forces called Amanto invades Japan and almost wipes out the entire Samurai soul of people living there. But there is one guy by the name Sakata Gintoki who still has the heart of samurai. The story follows Sakata Gintoki, a sweet tooth, poor,otaku and his 2 newfound friends Kagura, an amanto from Yato clan who has the strength of Gorilla and Shimura Shinpachi who has the dream of reviving his father's Dojo. The entire show revolves around these 3 trying to do anything for money and naming them "ODD JOBS".
